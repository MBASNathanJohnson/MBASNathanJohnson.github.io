Coro Liquid Gold is an innovative fundraising product for Schools. We offer 500g jars of delicious honey for your school to fundraise with. We have higher profit margins and are more healthy than most other competing fundraising products as well as highly personalised to fit your school and your local communities needs. We are also 100% New Zealand Owned and operated, and our honey is made, bottled and labelled in New Zealand.

Connecting to the local community is important for a fundraising product to succeed, so we are offering personalised labels to tailor to your local community and local projects, an example of a label with a community attachment may be the thing you are fundraising for (i.e. pool, local market, classrooms etc).
